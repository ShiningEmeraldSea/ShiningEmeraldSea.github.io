Cosmic Overtones

(check box to start, uncheck to end)


Overtone harpsichord keys: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 0, -, =, delete. Volume defaults to 0, but [, ], \ keys set to volume -1, volume 0, and volume 1. Overtone tabl works like this: 0h2v1l1-1h3v1l1-2h4v1l1 indicates playing the first harmonic after 0 seconds, playing the third harmonic after one second, then playing the fourth harmonic after 2 seconds. All of them at volume 1, all of them length of 1 second.
